The intention-to-treat analysis revealed significant improvements in depressive symptoms (d = 0.66),
generalized anxiety symptoms (d = 0.93), insomnia symptoms (d = 0.20), functional impairment (d =
0.22),andhealth-relatedqualityoflife(HRQoL;d=0.11)fromWeek0(baseline)toWeek9(immediatepost-

andstressmanagement)(d=0.40–0.89)andhigherlevelsoftotalactivity(d=0.55)andwalkingactivity(d=

nentLMinterventionLifestyleHubmayserveasaprimarymodalityformanagingdepressivesymptoms.
