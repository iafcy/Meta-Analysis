Introduction
With2billionpeopleaffectedglobally,1skinconditionsarealeadingcauseofmorbidity.The
examinationofsomeskinconditionsbydermatologistsresultsinsignificantlyhigherdiagnostic

Severalalgorithmsincorporatingartificialintelligence(AI)havebeendevelopedtohelp
