FollowinganMTBI,thereisnodoubtthatmanypeopleexperiencearangeofpostconcussionsymptoms(e.g.,headaches,diz-
ziness,fatigue,andcognitivedifficulties).Thesesymptomstypicallyresolvewithinthefirstfewdays,weeks,ormonthsformost
